More Than Just a Game

Step into the enchanting world of WildWing Haven, where you'll join a vibrant forest community working together to restore avian life in this magical realm. Experience the soothing tranquility of the forest while nurturing and collecting birds, from tiny hummingbirds to majestic parrots.

Aid the solitary tree spirit in summoning new birds and guide them from adorable chicks to magnificent adults. Clear overgrowth and let sunlight penetrate the forest floor to create the perfect habitat for your feathered friends to flourish.
